Grace H. Zhang

About

I am a quantitative researcher. You can reach me at mail.gracezhang (at) gmail (dot) com.

I completed my PhD in theoretical condensed matter physics at Harvard, where I was fortunate to work with David Nelson under the support of NSF GRFP and the Paul and Daisy Soros Fellowship. Previously, I obtained my undergraduate degree in physics at Massachusetts Institute of Technology and enjoyed a summer at Google Research, working with Anton Geraschenko on the applied science team.
